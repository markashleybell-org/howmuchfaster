How Much Faster?

This simple calculator (aimed at developers) gives you both percentage and multiplier improvement figures. It's not a complicated formula, but if you're anything like me, you have to look it up almost every time you need it...

Measurements can be any numeric value: response times, payload sizes in Kb/Mb, etc. The calculator works with increases as well as reductions, so you can also work out e.g. throughput improvements.
