How Much Faster?

A simple calculator (aimed at developers) to work out* percentage and multiplier improvement figures.

Measurements can be any numeric value: response times, payload sizes in Kb/Mb, etc. The calculator works with increases as well as reductions, so you can also work out e.g. throughput improvements.
