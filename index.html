How Much Faster?

A simple calculator for developers, which tells you how much faster/better you made something. Sure, it's not a complicated formula... but if you're anything like me, you have to look it up nearly every time you need it.

The measurements can be any numeric value: response times, payload sizes in Kb/Mb, etc. The calculator works with increases as well as reductions, so you can also work out e.g. throughput improvements.
